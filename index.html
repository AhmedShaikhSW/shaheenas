Scrambled Egg

Easy to make, delicious, and less time-consuming. The ideal breakfast for busy days.

Set.

Chop half an onion into small pieces. Similarly, chop half a tomato into small pieces. Cut one green chilli using scissors. Group the chopped tomatoes and cut chilli together and add a pinch o salt to them.

Break an egg into a small bowl. Add the same amount of salt to the egg as you added to the tomatoes and chilli. Add a pinch of red chilli powder. Beat the egg.

Cook!

Set the (karhai) atop medium flame. Add 2-3 tspns. vegetable oil. Add teh chopped onions.

Wait (1-2 mins.) for the onions to attain a golden brown color. Add the tomatoes and chilli together. Stir for 1 minute.

Now add the egg. Scrape the eggs from the sides using the metal spatula into the center. Use the sharp end to cut the egg into pieces repeatedly until satisfactory and cook it until it obtains a light yellow color.

Done!
